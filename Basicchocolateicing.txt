Categories: icing
Ingredients:
	120 g butter at room temperature - chopped
	1 1/3 cup icing sugar
	1/2 cup cocoa
	1 to 2 tsp milk

Instructions:
	Mix the butter and icing sugar and beat it in with an electric mixture.
	Add the cocoa and beat.
	Mix in the milk to get the desired consistency.

Notes: For butter icing omit the cocoa and use 2 1/2 cups of icing sugar.
