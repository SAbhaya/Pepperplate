Title: Chocolate Sauce
Description: 
Source: Taste.com.au

Yield: 
Active: 
Total: 
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/3229576.jpg
Ingredients:
	200 ml pure (thin) cream
	50 ml milk
	200 g dark chocolate, chopped
	20 g good-quality cocoa powder

Instructions:
	Place the cream and milk in a saucepan over medium heat. Bring to just below boiling point, then remove from heat and gently whisk in chocolate and cocoa until smooth. Set aside to cool.

Notes: Extra sauce will keep refrigerated for up to 5 days.
