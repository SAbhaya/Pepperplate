Title: Chocolate Chip Cookies
Description: The classic chocolate chip cookie is hard to resist. THis one has no added embellishments - just the traditional drop cookie chock full of its eponymous bits of chocolate and, of course, make with vagan ingredients.

Categories: cookies, vegan
Ingredients:
	1/2 cup vegan margarine, softened
	3/4 cup light brown sugar
	2 tbsp pure maple syrup
	1 tsp pure vanilla extract
	2 cups all purpose flour
	1 tsp baking soda
	1/2 tsp baking powder
	1/2 tsp salt
	1 cup semisweet vegan chocolate chips

Instructions:
	Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
	In a large bowl, cream together the margarine and sugar until light and fluffy. Stir in the maple syrup and vanilla and mix until smooth.
	In a separate large bowl, combine the flour, baking soda, baking powder and salt. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring well to combine. Gold in the chocolate chips.
	Drop the dough by the tablespoonful onto ungreased baking sheets. Bake until slightly browned around the edges, about 15 min. Cool for a few min before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely, about 20 min. When completely cool, store in an air tight container.

Notes: Liquid sweeteners - honey is not vegan, hence not used. Liquid vegan sweeteners include agave nectar, maple syrup, molasses and brown rice syrup.
NOTE: Dry and liquid sweeteners are not interchangeable in recipes.
