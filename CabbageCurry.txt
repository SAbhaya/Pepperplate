Title: Cabbage Curry
Description: 
Source: ape amma
Original URL: 
Yield: 
Active: 
Total: 
Categories: ape amma
Ingredients:
	300 g cabbage
	Coconut milk
	1/2 big onion
	1 tsp curry powder
	1/2 tsp turmeric powder
	1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
	2 garlic cloves
	3 green chilies
	2 Pandan leaves
	2 to 3 springs curry leaves
	1/4 tsp salt

Instructions:
	Cut and wash cabbage
	add curry leaves, curry powder, salt, turmeric, fenugreek seeds, Pandan leaves, green chilies, garlic cloves, diced onion
	mix together
	cook, low-medium heat, closed lid. 5 min
	mix, medium heat closed lid. 3 min
	cook until water evaporate
	remove curry leaves
	add coconut milk cook check the seasoning.
	cook until coconut milk boils, then off and remove.
