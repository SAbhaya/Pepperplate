Title: Avocado and Tuna Sushi Sandwiches

Ingredients:
	4 slices wholemeal bread, crusts removed
	2 tablespoons spreadable cream cheese
	95 g can tuna in springwater, drained
	1/2 medium avocado, peeled, thinly sliced
	1/2 medium carrot, peeled, grated

Instructions:
	Place bread slices on a chopping board. Using a rolling pin, flatten bread. Spread 1 side of each slice with cheese. Arrange tuna, avocado and carrot in rows next to each other on bread, leaving a 1cm border along 1 edge.
	Roll up bread from opposite edge to enclose filling. Cut each roll into 3 rounds. Serve.
