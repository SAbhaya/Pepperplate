Title: Chili Oil
Description: 
Source: Vegetarian Times
Original URL: http://www.vegetariantimes.com/recipe/chili-oil/
Yield: Makes 1/4 cup
Active: 
Total: 
Image: https://cdn2.pepperplate.com/recipes/3392905.jpg
Ingredients:
	1/4 cup peanut oil
	1 Tbs. red pepper flakes

Instructions:
	Heat oil in saucepan over medium-high heat. Add red pepper flakes, and cook until they sizzle. Pour oil into glass jar, cover, and cool to room temperature. Stir or shake before using.
